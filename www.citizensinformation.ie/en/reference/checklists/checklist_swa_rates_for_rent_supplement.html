Introduction

Use this checklist with the document ‘Calculating Rent Supplement’ and the worksheet for calculating Rent Supplement.

When a person applies for Rent Supplement, their means are assessed to work out how much Rent Supplement they qualify for. This amount of Rent Supplement will generally ensure that your income, after paying rent, does not fall below a minimum level. This level is the appropriate basic Supplementary Welfare Allowance (SWA) rate for your circumstances minus a minimum contribution. You must always pay at least €30 towards your rent and couples must pay €40. However, you may pay more depending on your means.

You can use this checklist to get the appropriate basic SWA rate for a range of circumstances.
