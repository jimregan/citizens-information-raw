Information

The Office of the Court of Appeal:

  • Provides administrative support to the Court of Appeal,
  • Processes appeals from decisions of the Central Criminal Court, the Circuit Criminal Court and the Special Criminal Court
  • Deals with courts martial appeals to the Court of Appeal

Further information

Further information is available from the Office of the Court of Appeal for criminal and courts martial appeals.
