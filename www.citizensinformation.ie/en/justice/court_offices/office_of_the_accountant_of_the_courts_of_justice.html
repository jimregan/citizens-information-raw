Information

The Accountant of the Courts of Justice

  • is responsible for the receipt, custody, investment and payment out of monies required to be lodged in court either by direction of the High Court or the Supreme Court, by statute or by the Rules of Court.
  • has custody of insurance bonds required to be entered into by all licensed auctioneers.
