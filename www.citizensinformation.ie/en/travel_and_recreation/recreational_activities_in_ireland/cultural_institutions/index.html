Cultural Institutions

National Museum of Ireland
The National Museum of Ireland is devoted to Irish archaeology and history, decorative arts and natural history. Information on museum locations, educational programmes and visitor information.

Irish Museum of Modern Art
The Irish Museum of Modern Art, is Ireland's leading national institution for contemporary art. Information on educational and outreach programmes and on the collections.

National Gallery of Ireland
The National Gallery of Ireland houses both the State's national collection of Irish art and the collection of European master paintings. Information on educational programmes offered by the Gallery and the collections.

Crawford Art Gallery
The Crawford Art Gallety's collection ranges from eighteenth century Irish and European painting and sculpture, through to contemporary video installations.

National Library of Ireland
This document describes the collection of and the services provided by the National Library of Ireland.

Chester Beatty Library
The Chester Beatty Library is located in Dublin and houses a collection of Oriental and Western manuscripts and art.

National Archives of Ireland
The National Archives of Ireland contains a huge volume of documents that help provide an understanding of the social, economic and political history of Ireland. Information on the collections and the serives offered by the National Archives of Ireland.

National Concert Hall
The National Concert Hall has hosted some of the world’s greatest performers.

Abbey Theatre
The Abbey Theatre in Dublin city is Ireland’s national theatre, founded in 1903.
