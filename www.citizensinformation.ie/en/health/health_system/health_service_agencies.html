Health agencies

  • Information

Information

There are a number of agencies and bodies that have a role in delivering health services. Some are involved in delivering services while others are regulatory and/or advisory.

There are currently 2 bodies in the health area which are the joint responsibility of the governments of Northern Ireland and Ireland, see Island of Ireland below.

Most healthcare is provided within the main structure of the Health Service Executive (HSE). Other agencies which it supports are listed below, as well as websites dealing with specific health issues.
