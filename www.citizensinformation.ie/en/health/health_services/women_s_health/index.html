Maternity and infant care services
The public health service provides free maternity services for all expectant mothers for the period of the pregnancy and for 6 weeks after the birth.

Fertility treatment
Assisted Human Reproduction Services (including IVF) are not provided by the public health services. Certain treatments are available and there are rules on the provision of these.

Crisis Pregnancy Programme
The main function of the Crisis Pregnancy Programme is to prepare a strategy to address crisis pregnancy. It provides information and contacts for counselling and support services.

Abortion information - the law
Abortion is illegal in Ireland except in very strict circumstances. There are laws on how information about foreign abortion services may be made available.
