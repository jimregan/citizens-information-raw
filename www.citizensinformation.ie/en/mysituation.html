This page is divided into two sections, "I am..." and "I want to...".

"I am..." contains links to information for people in particular situations, for example, people with a disability or people starting a business. Each link in the section goes to either an overview document, a checklist of services and entitlements or a part of the website with relevant information.

"I want to..." contains links that aim to answer Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) on this website, for example, how to apply for a driving licence or how to appeal a Social Welfare decision. Each link in this section goes to a document or a part of the website with relevant information.
