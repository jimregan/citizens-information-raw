After-School Child Care Scheme
If you have been unemployed and you get a new job, increase the number of hours that you work or take up a place on an employment support scheme you may be eligible for a subsidised afterschool childcare place for primary school children.

Back to Work Allowance
The Back to Work Allowance Scheme provides support to certain unemployed people (and others) when they return to work. The scheme provides a continued partial payment of social welfare entitlements and 'secondary benefits'. From 1 May 2009, the scheme is closed to new applicants.

Part-Time Job Incentive Scheme

Short-Term Enterprise Allowance
This scheme provides a weekly payment to certain people who have lost their job and want to start their own business.
